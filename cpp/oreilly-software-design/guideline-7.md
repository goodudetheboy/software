# Guideline 7: Understand the Similarities Between Base Classes and Concepts

Author's remark: LSP is not just limited to dynamic polymorphism, but also static polymorphism and templated code.

Whether it's class-based or concepts (C++20 idea of static polymorphism), they all share **expected behaviors**, aka, abstraction. Therefore, this would require all of them to follow LSP.

> Whichever you pick, the LSP still applies
